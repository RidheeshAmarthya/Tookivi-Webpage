Social Awareness Program

Social Awareness Program or SAP is a very good initiative taken by the authorities of Amity to create awareness about topics not spoken about enough, sensitive topics, social topics, etc. Universities shouldnâ€™t just stick to pure academical teaching, and this workshop is a really effective way of including that missing co-curricular activity, while staying relevant at the same time.  

Semester 6

We have had this project every semester ever since joining the university, and as a consequence we have learnt a lot about various social issues. We have interacted with many different people from different communities, lifestyles, classes, and cultures. Going through all different perspectives and thought processes of various people we have gained an immense amount of knowledge and have put across our point to them.
We have spoken to people about topics including forest fires, exam anxiety, bullying, and terrorism. We spread awareness about things people did not know about, explained different aspects of our project, and made them understand our topics well. 

Social Distancing During The Pandemic

Unfortunately, due to Covid-19, we were unable to physically interact with the public, though regardless we fully hope to convey our message through the survey we have prepared this time. We are very thankful to Amity University for giving us this wonderful opportunity and we wish to carry forward this practice later on in life and beyond our college life.
